Strategies

Here's a list of stock screener strategies you can test to scan for stocks to potentially swing trade.

Strategy - EMASMARSI

This is a simple strategy, based on simple moving average which is always profitable and trades better than most premium channels.

When to buy?

  • Weekly RSI(30) ≥ daily RSI(30) > 70
  • MA(10) > MA(20) > MA(50) > MA(100)

When to sell?

  • Daily close ≥ 2 percent below MA(10)
  • 8 percent fixed stop loss is hit
